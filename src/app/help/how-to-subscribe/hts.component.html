Amazon Prime Members Can Get It For FREE

1. On the Twitch Web Player Do Not Click the Subscribe Purple Button. Instead you want to click this Twitch Logo in the bottom right corner.

This will take you to Twitch's website.

2. Just above the Twtich Web Player there is a purple 'Subscribe' button. Click it and you will see a list of Subscription Tiers. If you are not an Amazon Prime member or have not linked your Amazon Prime account you will not see the Free Subscribe button.

3. Click the 'Subscribe Free' button

You can subscribe using any Subscription Tier. You will be rewarded relative to the Tier you subscribe with.
